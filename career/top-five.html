Top Five Strengths

1. Arranger
  • I am resourceful; I will find a way to get it to work.
  • I thrive in situations where I have many things going on at the same time.
  • I am flexible, in how I organize people and how I configure space.
  • I need to be in a dynamic area of an organization. Routine, static part of an organization drains my energy.
  • I am always looking for the perfect configuration.
Action!

Ideas For Action

  1. Be sure to keep track of ongoing deadlines for your many tasks, projects, and obligations. Although you enjoy the chance to juggle lots activities, others with less powerful Arranger talents may become anxious if they don't see you working on their projects frequently. Inform them of your progress to ease their fears.
  2. Seek complex, dynamic environments in which there are few routines.
  3. Take on the organization of a big event; a convention, a large party, or a company celebration.
  4. Give people time to understand your way of doing things when you present it to them. Your mental juggling is instinctive, but others might find it difficult to break with existing procedures. Take the time to clearly explain why your way can be more effective.
  5. Figure out how you can improve workflow by rearranging space and/or procedures to maximize efficiency and to free up time for you and for others.
2. Learner

I am eager to learn new facts, skills or knowledge.
I seek roles that requires some form of technical competence.
I prefer fields with constantly changing technologies or regulations.
I am drawn to the process of learning rather than the content or the result.

3. Adaptability

I live in the moment. I don't resent sudden requests.
I am a very flexible person that can stay productive when demands of work are pulling me in many directions at once.
I enjoy roles which success depends on responding to constantly changing circumstances.
I try to avoid tasks that are too structured and stifle my need for variety.
I am most productive on short term assignments that requires immediate action.
I prefer life with quick skirmishes rather than long drawn out campaigns.

4. Empathy

I am sensitive to the emotions of others / I can sense the emotions of those around me.
I can easily gauge the emotional tone of a room; I have the talent to forge a bridge of understanding and mutual support.
People are drawn to me because I help them give voice to their emotional life.
I hear the unvoiced questions, and I anticipate the needs of others.

5. Individualization

I am intrigued by the unique qualities of each person, I focus on the differences between individuals.
Because of my keen observation of other's strengths, I can draw out the best in each person, which helps me build productive teams.
I move comfortably around broad range of style and cultures, and intuitively personalize my interaction.
I am easily able to understand someone else's perspective.
